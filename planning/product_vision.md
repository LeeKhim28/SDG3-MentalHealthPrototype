# Product Vision – MindCare

## Problem Statement
Many young people under 30 experience stress, anxiety, and emotional pressure from studies, work, and personal life. However, they may not know where to seek help or feel uncomfortable asking for support.

## Solution
MindCare is a mental health support prototype that provides simple tools such as mood tracking, self-check tests, and access to help resources.

## Target Users
- University students
- Young working adults
- Individuals under 30 with emotional stress

## Project Goal
To design a simple and user-friendly prototype that improves awareness and provides early mental health support.
